We started Qrated when a venue laughed off our proposal to organise an indie hip hop gig in Delhi.




Six days later they had to take out their furniture to make room for a crowd they had never seen before. .




Our team has cut its teeth breaking conventions in pulling off Indie Hip Hop gigs. With a completely hands on approach and laser sharp focus on details, we know how to organise an otherwise mundane hip-hop gig to an experience.
